## [1.0.1] - 8-Oct-2024
### Added
- New macro **Specific Weakness & Resistance** that allows the GM to manually select damage types and values for resistances and weaknesses for selected tokens.
- Introduced the **Random Weaknesses & Resistances*2** macro, which applies higher values of resistances and weaknesses (2-10 based on level) for a greater challenge.
  
### Changed
- Cleaned up and optimized the existing macros, improving performance and clarity.
